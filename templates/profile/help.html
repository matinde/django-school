Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

How can I update my profile information?

Go to Settings in your profile menu. Here you can update your personal information, contact details, and notification preferences.

How do I contact my teacher?

Use the messaging system to send a direct message to your teacher. You can also view teacher contact information on their profile page.

Where can I find my assignments?

Click on the "Assignments" tab in your dashboard to view all current and upcoming assignments. You can filter by subject or due date.
