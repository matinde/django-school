Getting Started

Welcome to the School Management System

Here's how to get started with the basic features:

For Teachers:

  • View your assigned classes and students
  • Create and manage assignments
  • Communicate with parents
  • Track student progress

For Students:

  • View your class schedule
  • Access assignments and study materials
  • Submit completed work
  • Track your grades

For Parents:

  • Monitor your children's progress
  • Communicate with teachers
  • View upcoming assignments
  • Access school announcements

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your profile page by clicking on your name in the top menu, then click "Edit Profile".

How do I contact a teacher?

Use the messaging system to send a direct message to any teacher in your or your child's class.

Where can I find my assignments?

Click on "Assignments" in the main menu to view all your current and past assignments.
